Contribute

Virginia Polyamory is 100% volunteer run and supported.

We produce and attend events for outreach to improve public relations and spread education about polyamory.
If you have found resources, help, and community here that was helpful and positive, we hope you can give something back.

Volunteer

Got a special skill that might be helpful? We'd love to hear about it!
Send us a message if you would like to volunteer your time or abilities.

Donate

Unfortunately, we are not currently accepting general financial donations online.
You can still make a tangible impact! Contact us about ways to contribute to specific purposes,
such as providing refreshments for a meeting, books for the library, or helping to pay for event registration fees.
